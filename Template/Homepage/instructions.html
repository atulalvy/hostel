STAGE 1 : REGISTRATION
  • In the Sign Up section (which can be found in the next page), Enter the Email Address and a password of choice
  • Ignore the '.' characters before '@', if any in the email address.
  • The password must contain alphabets, at least a single digit or a special character and must contain at least 8 characters.
  • A one time link for the email verification will be received in the provided email address. Click on the link to verify the account.
  • Login to the account to apply.
STAGE 2 : APPLICATION
  • All fields except CAT rank are mandatory.
  • The pincode,state and district fields corresponds to your permanent address.
  • The year of study refers to the upcoming year of study in the university for the corresponding course.
  • If there are any discrepancies regarding the category, It would be in default considered as General.
  • Dates of admission and completion requires that minimum the year should be relatively correct.
  • Physically disabled candidates and those who belong to the Prime Ministers Special Education Program should be registered with the University as such.
  • On clicking the submit button after filling the details, the entered details can be viewed in the views section.
  • The profile photo should be a passport size photo.
  • The details if any correction can be overwritten by applying again with the correct details.
  • PhD and MPhil students are excluded from the time frame limit specified. They can apply even after the regulated time frame.
